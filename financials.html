Working Financial Model — Directional Only
This model reflects our current operating plan as of April 2026. It is directional, not a formal projection. DxD operates in a fast-moving market with rapidly evolving partnerships, pipeline, and product timelines — this model is updated continuously as conditions change. Key revenue lines are modeled conservatively: multiple identified revenue streams are held at $0 pending contract execution. Treat these numbers as a working framework for how we think about the business, not a commitment.
