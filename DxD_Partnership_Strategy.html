Public Safety & Post-Sales Operations
"How do you expand TAM beyond direct sales?"
Many companies in the drone, sensor, and public safety space sell hardware but can't operate it. DxD becomes the operations layer — providing post-sale services, training, and managed operations for their customers. This expands our TAM without new hardware development and creates recurring service revenue on top of existing product sales.
