03 / The Origin

The company begins with an operational wound, not a market map.

2010 / Afghanistan

A platoon pinned down in a high-stress ambush. Air support saved men on the ground. The next morning, 52 civilian casualties were reported. The lesson: the tools at point of contact decide how the next tragedy is written.

2018 / Las Vegas

After Mandalay Bay, Pat asked the LVMPD chief what would have happened if a TASER drone could have reached the window in under a minute. The answer: over 50 lives would have been saved.

2022 / Uvalde

Working prototypes were not enough. Responsible operators, law enforcement coordination, and a unified system mattered as much as the technology.

"The answer was not just technology. The answer was responsible operators, coordinating with law enforcement, inside a unified system."
